Our Story in 3D

Every promise is a milestone. As we journey forward together, we vow to keep our hearts intertwined. This space is dedicated to the little promises we make and keep, forever.

How It Works

🔗

1. Connect

Create a room and securely pair with your partner using a unique Connection Code or QR scan.

💌

2. Promise

Make a promise. Set a time limit. Your partner must authorize the vow before it becomes official.

✨

3. Fulfill

Mark promises as Kept (Delulu) or Broken. Keep track of your milestones together.

🎉

4. Celebrate

Look back at a beautiful timeline of all the promises you've fulfilled for each other.

Features

🔒 Secure Authentication

Dual-password protection ensures only you and your partner can update your promises.

📱 Cross-Device Realtime

Instant synchronization. When one partner makes a vow, the other gets a live prompt to approve it.

⏳ Timed Vows

Promises automatically expire and break if they aren't fulfilled within your chosen timeframe.

🎨 Romantic 3D UI

Immersive, highly animated glassmorphism and claymorphism aesthetic.

🔔 Instant Notifications

Get visual prompts exactly when your partner interacts with your shared room.

☁️ Cloud Synced

All your data is saved automatically. Access your promises from anywhere.
